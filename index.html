Popular Garnishes

Add excitement and flair with just a few simple ingredients.

  • Fruit Skewers

    Simply cut the fruit or cocktail cherries into small pieces and thread them onto the skewers in any pattern,rest the skewer on the rim of your glass or use it to stir your drink.

  • Chocolate Shavings

    Peel thin shavings from the chocolate bar,sprinkle the shavings over the surface of your drink for a luxurious and delicious touch.

  • Spiced Sugar

    Granulated sugar, ground cinnamon, nutmeg, or any other spice,mix the sugar and spice together, dip the rim of your glass in a shallow dish of water or citrus juice, followed by the spiced sugar mixture.

  • Cucumber Ribbons

    Peel a long, thin strip of cucumber from the outside edge, curl into a spiral shape and Drape over the rim of your glass.

  • Herb Sprigs

    Using rosemary,thyme,or mint,tie it together with twine or string,drape the sprig over the rim of glass.

  • Flower Petals

    Edible flowers adding a natural organic touch with unique and diverse flavours.
